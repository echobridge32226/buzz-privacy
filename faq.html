Languages & Accuracy
  • 40+ languages across four AI providers. Western languages (English, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Chinese, Korean, Arabic, and more) use Deepgram. Indic languages (Hindi, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Marathi, Gujarati, Punjabi, and more) use Sarvam AI. Some additional languages go through Gladia. Full list here →
  • Yep. Free plan is English-only (US, India, UK, Australia). Basic and Pro open up all 40+ languages.
  • Translation is a paid feature. Basic plan translates to English only. Pro translates to any available language - Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Chinese, Korean, Arabic, Portuguese, Hindi, all Indic languages via Sarvam, and more. Free plan doesn't include translation.
  • For Indic languages like Hindi, Tamil, or Bengali, you can choose native script (e.g., हिन्दी) or Roman transliteration (e.g., "Mera naam Rohit hai"). Great if you understand the language but don't read the script. Both use Sarvam's saaras:v3 model.
  • Depends a lot on the language, your accent, and background noise. In good conditions, Deepgram Nova-3 is very accurate for English and major Western languages; Sarvam does well for Indic languages. It's best-effort - don't use it for anything legally binding, medical, or safety-critical. You can always tap to fix any segment that got garbled.
  • Yes! If your conversation hops between languages, pick "Multi-language" in the language picker. Gladia handles up to 10 language hints for code-switching, and Deepgram and Google have their own multi-language detection modes too.
Sessions & Features
  • Three types: Solo (just you and a mic), Group (up to 10 people, everyone on their own phone, joined via QR code or a 6-character code), and Voice Note (upload a recording and get the transcript back).
  • Up to 4 hours per session, regardless of your plan. If you hit the cap, the session ends automatically. Just start a new one for the rest.
  • MP3, M4A, AAC, OGG (WhatsApp and Telegram), WAV, WebM, FLAC, CAF (iMessage/iOS), and MP4. Max file size is 50 MB. Buzz picks the right AI provider automatically based on the language you select.
  • Yes! Tap any segment in your history to edit it. Fix a word, correct a speaker name, delete something. Saves automatically and shows up on all your devices.
  • Speaker labels work for all Western languages processed by Deepgram (English, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, etc.). Not available for Indic languages - that's a Sarvam AI limitation, not something we can work around right now.
  • No - Buzz needs internet for everything. Transcription, translation, history, account management - all online. No offline mode.
  • iPhone (iOS 16+) and Android (7.0+) only right now. No desktop or web app.
Export & Sharing
  • Export and sharing is Pro only. You get plain text sharing and polished PDF export - speaker labels, timestamps, word count, and full multi-script font support. Free and Basic can't export.
  • Yes! Buzz uses Google's Noto Sans for PDFs, so it covers Devanagari, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Bengali, Gujarati, Gurmukhi, Arabic, Thai, Sinhala, Khmer, Myanmar, Georgian, Hebrew, CJK (Chinese/Japanese/Korean), Latin - the works.
  • Pro plan only. Use the share button to send as plain text via WhatsApp, email, Messages, whatever. Or export a PDF. No public session links - everything stays in-app.
Privacy & Data
  • Never. Audio passes through server memory only - streamed to the AI provider and gone the moment your session ends. Nothing hits a hard drive. Ever.
  • Yes. Transcript text is encrypted with AES-256 before it's stored. The key lives server-side and never touches the app.
  • Free keeps 7 days, Basic keeps 30, Pro keeps 90. Sessions older than your limit get automatically deleted - transcripts, speaker labels, the lot. You can also delete any session yourself at any time.
  • Go to the Delete Account page or Settings → Profile → Delete Account in the app. We'll wipe everything - sessions, transcripts, usage data, your account - within 7 days.
Billing & Credits
  • New sessions pause until your quota resets at midnight your time. Got credits? They kick in automatically so you don't have to stop. No credits? You'll see a prompt to grab a pack or upgrade.
  • One-time purchases that add extra transcription time. Works on any plan - credits kick in after your included quota runs out. Available in 15, 60, 120, and 300-minute packs. Expire 60 days after purchase, non-refundable.
  • Cancel in your App Store or Google Play account settings. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period, then drop back to Free. We don't handle billing - that's all Apple or Google.
  • Refunds are handled by Apple or Google - not us. For subscriptions, submit a request through the App Store or Google Play. Credit packs are non-refundable once purchased.
  • Credits kick in during a session when your plan quota runs out - not when you start. So if you've hit your limit but have credits, you can still start a new session fine. Seeing "limit reached"? Double-check your credits haven't expired (60 days from purchase).
