Delete Your Buzz Data

Last updated: April 19, 2026

You can delete some or all of your data in Buzz at any time — without having to delete your account. This page explains your options and exactly what data is deleted or retained in each case.

Option 1: Delete Individual Sessions In-App Instant

How to delete a single session

  1. Open the Buzz app and go to your session history.
  2. Swipe left on the session you want to delete, or tap into it and select Delete.
  3. Confirm the deletion. The session transcript and metadata are permanently removed immediately.

Option 2: Delete All Session Data via Email Within 7 days

If you want all of your session transcripts and metadata deleted at once — while keeping your account active — send us an email request.

How to request bulk session deletion

  1. Send an email to echobridge32226@gmail.com from the address associated with your Buzz account.
  2. Use the subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  3. In the body, specify that you want all session data deleted but wish to keep your account.
  4. We will process your request and confirm deletion within 7 days.
