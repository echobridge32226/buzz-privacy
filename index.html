Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Buzz ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Buzz app ("App").

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • Your name (if provided via Google Sign-In)

Audio

When you use live captioning, your device's microphone audio is streamed in real time to our servers for transcription. Audio is not recorded or stored. It is processed in memory and discarded immediately after transcription.

Transcripts

The text output of your captioning sessions (transcripts) is stored in our database so you can access your session history. You can delete your sessions at any time from within the App.

Usage Data

We do not collect analytics, crash reports, or behavioral tracking data beyond what is necessary to operate the App.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Authenticate you and maintain your account
  • Provide real-time speech-to-text captioning
  • Store and display your session history
  • Operate and improve the App

Third-Party Services

Buzz uses the following third-party services that may process your data:

ServicePurposePrivacy Policy
Deepgram Speech-to-text transcription (receives audio stream) deepgram.com/privacy
Firebase (Google) Authentication and data storage firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Audio sent to Deepgram is subject to Deepgram's privacy policy. Deepgram does not use your audio to train models unless you have separately opted in to their data sharing program.

Data Retention

  • Account data is retained until you delete your account
  • Session transcripts are retained until you delete them, or until you delete your account
  • Audio is never stored — it is processed in real time and discarded

Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Delete individual sessions from within the App
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data — see our Account Deletion page

Children's Privacy

Buzz is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
