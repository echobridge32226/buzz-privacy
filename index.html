Features

Built for the moments

Whether you're in a noisy meeting, a foreign country, or a family dinner where everyone's talking at once - Buzz keeps you in the loop.

Live Captions

Words show up on screen as they're being said - not after. Like having subtitles for real life.

Live Translation

Someone speaking a different language? No problem. Read the captions in yours, in real time, as they talk.

Speaker Diarization

Buzz figures out who said what and color-codes them. Tap any label to rename "Speaker 1" to "Mum". The names stick.

Voice Note Import

Got a 3-minute WhatsApp voice note you can't listen to right now? Drop it into Buzz and read it instead. Done.

Session History

Every conversation saved and searchable. Fix anything that got garbled, rename speakers, scroll back to what was said. It's your history.

Your audio vanishes the moment a session ends. Transcripts stay encrypted. Your conversations are yours - full stop.
