Live Captions

Real-time speech-to-text streamed over WebSocket with sub-second latency. Powered by Deepgram Nova-3, Sarvam, and Gladia.

Live Translation

Translate captions in real time to English, Spanish, French, Japanese, Hindi, Arabic, and more — via DeepL, Sarvam, and Google.

Speaker Diarization

Automatically identify and label multiple speakers. Rename "Speaker 1" to "Alice" — labels persist across your session history.

Upload MP3, M4A, OGG, WAV, CAF, and more — up to 50 MB. Transcribe WhatsApp voice messages, iMessage audio, or recordings.

Every session is saved and searchable. Edit transcript segments, fix names, add corrections — your history, your words.

Audio is never stored. Transcripts are encrypted at rest with AES-256. No data sold, no AI training on your content.
