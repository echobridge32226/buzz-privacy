Delete Your Buzz Account

Last updated: April 19, 2026

You can request deletion of your Buzz account and all associated data at any time. This page explains how to submit a deletion request, and exactly what data is deleted or retained.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to echobridge32226@gmail.com from the email address associated with your Buzz account.
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include your registered email address in the body so we can identify your account.
  4. We will process your request and confirm deletion within 7 days.

Ready to submit your request? Tap the button below to open a pre-filled email.

Email Us to Delete Account

What Data Is Deleted

Upon processing your deletion request, we will permanently delete:

DataActionRetention After Request
Your account credentials (email, sign-in info) Deleted None - deleted within 7 days
All captioning session records and transcripts Deleted None - deleted within 7 days
Session metadata (title, date, word count, language) Deleted None - deleted within 7 days
Audio N/A - never stored Audio is processed in real time and never recorded or persisted

What Data May Be Retained

We do not retain any personal data after account deletion. No data is kept for analytical or commercial purposes.

Note: Backups held by our infrastructure providers (Firebase / Google Cloud) may retain deleted data for up to 30 additional days as part of their standard backup rotation before it is permanently purged from all systems.

Questions

If you have any questions about this process, contact us at echobridge32226@gmail.com.
