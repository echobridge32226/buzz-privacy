# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 11, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Buzz ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Buzz app ("App").

---

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Your email address
- Your name (if provided via Google Sign-In)

### Audio
When you use live captioning, your device's microphone audio is streamed in real time to our servers for transcription. **Audio is not recorded or stored.** It is processed in memory and discarded immediately after transcription.

### Transcripts
The text output of your captioning sessions (transcripts) is stored in our database so you can access your session history. You can delete your sessions at any time from within the App.

### Usage Data
We do not collect analytics, crash reports, or behavioral tracking data beyond what is necessary to operate the App.

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Authenticate you and maintain your account
- Provide real-time speech-to-text captioning
- Store and display your session history
- Operate and improve the App

---

## Third-Party Services

Buzz uses the following third-party services that may process your data:

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---|---|---|
| **Deepgram** | Speech-to-text transcription (receives audio stream) | [deepgram.com/privacy](https://deepgram.com/privacy) |
| **Gladia** | Speech-to-text transcription for certain languages (receives audio stream) | [gladia.io/privacy-policy](https://www.gladia.io/privacy-policy) |
| **Sarvam AI** | Speech-to-text transcription for Indic languages (receives audio stream) | [sarvam.ai/privacy](https://www.sarvam.ai/privacy) |
| **DeepL** | Transcript translation (receives transcript text) | [deepl.com/privacy](https://www.deepl.com/privacy) |
| **Firebase (Google)** | Authentication and data storage | [firebase.google.com/support/privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy) |
| **RevenueCat** | Subscription and in-app purchase management (receives purchase receipts and subscription status) | [revenuecat.com/privacy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy) |

Audio sent to Deepgram, Gladia, or Sarvam AI is used solely for transcription and is subject to each provider's privacy policy. None of these providers use your audio to train models without your explicit consent. Transcript text sent to DeepL for translation is not stored beyond the immediate translation request.

---

## Data Retention

- **Account data** is retained until you delete your account
- **Session transcripts** are retained until you delete them, or until you delete your account
- **Audio** is never stored — it is processed in real time and discarded

---

## Your Rights

You may at any time:
- Delete individual sessions from within the App
- Request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email below

## Children's Privacy

Buzz is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.
