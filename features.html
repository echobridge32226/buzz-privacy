Core

Captions that keep
up with you

Your words appear on screen before you've even finished saying them - not after. The gap between speaking and reading is under a second, and it stays that way throughout the whole session.

WebSocket binary streaming - continuous, not push-to-talk
Deepgram Nova-3 for English and major Western languages
Sarvam saaras:v3 for 23 Indic languages (Hindi, Tamil, Bengali, and more)
Gladia for multilingual code-switching sessions (up to 10 language hints)
Per-segment confidence scores for quality feedback
Flip for other person - one tap rotates captions 180° so the person across from you can read them face-on (solo sessions only)
Auto-scroll - transcript scrolls to the latest caption as words arrive, keeping you at the bottom without touching the screen
Background captioning - transcription continues when you switch to another app or lock your screen

Translation

Read it in your language,
whatever they're speaking

Diarization

Know who said
what, when

Buzz listens for different voices, separates them, and color-codes them automatically. Tap any label to rename "Speaker 2" to "Mum". Those names stick in your session history so future transcripts actually make sense.

Powered by Deepgram for all Western and English sessions
Enabled by default - toggle off if not needed
Custom speaker names persist in transcript and PDF export
Always-on for voice note uploads via Deepgram (non-Indic) - Sarvam is used for Indic languages
Not available for Indic languages (Sarvam limitation)

Privacy

Your conversations
stay yours

Your audio doesn't touch a hard drive - ever. It streams to the AI and disappears. Transcripts sit encrypted in your account. We don't sell your data. That's just how it works.

Audio passes through server memory only - never written to disk

All connections over HTTPS/TLS
