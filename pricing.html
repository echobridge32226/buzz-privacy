  • Credits expire 60 days from the date of purchase. The expiry date is shown in the app when you purchase a pack.
  • Yes. Cancel through your App Store or Google Play account settings. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period, then automatically drop to the Free plan.
  • Yes, subscriptions renew monthly through the App Store or Google Play. Billing is handled entirely by Apple or Google — we never see your payment details.
