🍽️ Dinner Table

No one left out of
the conversation

"Dinner table syndrome" is the experience many deaf and hard-of-hearing people know too well — sitting at a family meal, surrounded by conversation, but unable to follow it. Buzz's group sessions change that.

Everyone at the table opens Buzz on their own phone and joins the same group session. Each person speaks into their own device — their voice is transcribed in real time and appears as captions on every screen. One conversation, everyone included.

Each person speaks into their own phone — no shared microphone needed
Up to 10 people can join a single session via QR code or join code
Every speaker's voice is transcribed separately and labelled
Works across languages — each participant can speak in their own language
Adjustable text size so captions are easy to read at a glance
