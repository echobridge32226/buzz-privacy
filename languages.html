Indic languages powered by Sarvam offer a transliteration option that renders speech in Roman/Latin script instead of the native script.

Translation

When translation is enabled for a session, captions are translated in real time to your chosen target language. For sessions transcribed in an Indic language, translation to English is handled by Sarvam with Google Translate as fallback. All other language pairs route to DeepL or Google Translate automatically.
